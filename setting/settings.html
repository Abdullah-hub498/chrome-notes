About Us

Boost your productivity with i Notes, a simple and efficient Chrome extension that lets you manage notes effortlessly.

  • ➕ Add & Delete Notes – Easily create, edit, and remove notes as needed.
  • 📝 One-Click Copy – Instantly copy notes to your clipboard.
  • ➕ Sort Notes – Sort notes.
  • ➕ Search Notes – Filter notes from the list.
  • 🌙 Dark & Light Mode – Customize your experience with a theme that suits your preference.
